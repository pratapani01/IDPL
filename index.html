About IDPL

Where Medicine Meets The Cricket Pitch

The Indian Doctor Premier League (IDPL) is India's first-of-its-kind cricket tournament designed exclusively for doctors, surgeons, residents, and all medical professionals. We believe your passion for cricket shouldn't stop when you wear the white coat.

IDPL is a national-level platform bringing together the brightest medical minds from across India — not just to compete on the field, but to connect, network, and celebrate the spirit of sport beyond the clinic walls.

This is more than a tournament. It's a movement — a celebration of two of India's greatest loves: Medicine and Cricket.

🏥 Doctors Only 🇮🇳 National Platform 🏆 Competitive Cricket 🤝 Professional Network

First-of-Its-Kind in India

An exclusive cricket league dedicated to medical professionals — a concept that has never been seen at this scale in India before.

🌐

National-Level Platform

Trials across 20+ cities, bringing together doctors from all specialties and all states under one cricketing umbrella.

Medical + Cricket DNA

Precision, discipline, and teamwork — values that define great doctors and great cricketers. IDPL is where both worlds collide.

How It Works

Registration Process

Four clear steps from your clinic to the cricket field.

📋

Doctor Registration

Fill in your details via the Google Form or WhatsApp. Pay the registration fee (Batter/Bowler ₹2,500 | All Rounder ₹3,500). Only verified medical professionals (MBBS, MD, MS, BDS, etc.) are eligible.

Trial Phase

Attend the cricket trial in your city. Showcase your batting, bowling, or all-round skills in front of IDPL selectors. Trials are conducted at designated grounds across 20+ cities.
